Yumcrumbs - Baking Business

The Journey of Yumcrumbs

Yumcrumbs started as a hobby in my kitchen, and it quickly turned into a thriving business, thanks to all the love and support from my friends, family, and community! I've baked over 5000+ orders and enjoyed every moment of it! From custom cakes to brownies, Yumcrumbs is all about making life a little sweeter.

FSSAI Registered Business

In order to ensure the highest safety and quality standards, I registered Yumcrumbs with the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). This certification allowed me to grow the business, ensuring that every treat I made complied with food safety regulations. This official recognition helped build trust with customers and gave me the opportunity to expand even further.

The Secret to Success

What makes Yumcrumbs special is the care and creativity I bring into every order. Baking isn't just about the ingredients; it's about the joy it brings to my customers. Whether it's a custom cake for a special occasion or a classic treat, Yumcrumbs stands for quality and creativity. My brownies, in particular, are a customer favorite, and I love experimenting with new flavors to make each order special.

Expansion and Growth

Over the years, Yumcrumbs has grown from a small hobby into a full-fledged business. With more than 5000 orders and a steady customer base, I’m proud of what Yumcrumbs has become. From special orders for birthdays to bulk orders for events, I’ve had the chance to bring my passion for baking to life for so many people.

Brownies: A Crowd Favorite

Brownies are a major hit among my customers. The feedback has been overwhelming, and I’ve created a wide variety of flavors! My favorite part is hearing how much people love the creativity and care that goes into each batch. From classic chocolate brownies to unique flavors, Yumcrumbs offers something for everyone!

Baking is My Therapy

Baking has always been my way of expressing creativity and finding calm in a busy world. It’s not just about business; it’s about bringing joy to others through something that brings me joy. Every order comes with a piece of my heart, and I’m so grateful to my customers who have made this journey possible.

Thank you for being a part of Yumcrumbs!
