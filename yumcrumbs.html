Yumcrumbs - Baking Business

The Journey of Yumcrumbs

Yumcrumbs started as a fun hobby in my kitchen, but it quickly turned into a thriving business thanks to all the love and support from my friends, family, and community! I've baked over 5000+ orders and enjoyed every moment of it! From custom cakes to brownies, Yumcrumbs is all about making life a little sweeter.

The Secret to Success

What makes Yumcrumbs special is the care and creativity I bring into every order. Baking isn't just about the ingredients; it's about the joy it brings to my customers. My brownies are a customer favorite, but every dessert I make is crafted with love. Whether it's a custom cake for a special occasion or a classic treat, Yumcrumbs stands for quality and creativity.

Expansion and Growth

Over the years, Yumcrumbs has grown from a small hobby into a full-fledged business. With more than 5000 orders and a steady customer base, I'm proud to say that what started as a personal project has become a part of so many celebrations and special moments.

Brownies: A Crowd Favorite

Brownies quickly became our best-selling item. The feedback from customers was amazing, and we've created a wide variety of flavors! My favorite part is hearing how much my customers love the creativity I pour into each batch. From classic chocolate to unique and adventurous combinations, Yumcrumbs is proud to offer something for everyone.

Baking is My Therapy

Baking has always been my way of expressing creativity and finding calm in a busy world. It's not just about business; it's about bringing joy to others through something that brings me joy. Every order comes with a piece of my heart, and I'm so grateful to all my customers who have made this journey possible.

Thank you for being a part of Yumcrumbs!
